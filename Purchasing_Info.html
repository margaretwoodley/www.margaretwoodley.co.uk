Purchasing Information

Post and packing is Free on orders up to £15

£15.01 - £ 50 p&p £1.50

Orders above £50 £3.00


Paypal is used to take payment however  YOU DO NOT NEED A PAYPAL ACCOUNT TO MAKE A PURCHASE.  Use your credit or debit card on the Paypal Page.


Step by step guide for using Paypal.


  1. 1.Firstly decide what post cards you want to purchase.

  2. 2.Enter the number required.

  3. 3.Then click the add to cart button.

  4. 4.You will be taken to the Paypal website where it will show your order.

  5. 5.If you want to add other cards to your order click the continue shopping button which will return you to the previous page.

  6. 6.If you want to finish shopping and pay and you are not on the Paypal website, click on the view cart button at the top of any page of margaretwoodley.co.uk.

  7. 7.This will take you to the Paypal website, then click proceed to checkout. This will take you to another web page.

  8. 8.If you have a Paypal account log into your account, which is on the right hand side of the page. This will then take you to your account where you will be able to pay.

  9. 9.If you do not have a Paypal account click on ‘continue’ on the left hand column. This will take you another page where you will be able to enter your credit or debit card details.
